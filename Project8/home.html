Lana Del Rey

Summary

Elizabeth Woolridge Grant (born June 21, 1985), known professionally as Lana Del Rey, is an American singer, songwriter, record producer, poet, model and music video director. Del Rey first performed under her real name Lizzy Grant but found Lana Del Rey in 2011 with a homemade music video for the song “Video Games.” After “Video Games” became a popular hit, she created viral albums such as Ultraviolence released in 2014, and Lust for Life released in 2017 with both landing No. 1 in Billboard 200.
